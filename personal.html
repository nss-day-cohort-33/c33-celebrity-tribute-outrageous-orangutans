Dwayne Douglas Johnson was born May 2, 1972 in Hayward, California to Rocky Johnson and Ata Johnson. He currently resides in a $5.5 million home in Southwest Ranches, Florida. He was married to Dany Garcia for 10 years. He is in a current relationship with Lauren Hashian. He has three beautiful daugthers, Simone, Jasmine and Tiana.

Dany Garcia (married 1996-2007): Garcia met Johnson during her undergraduate days at the University of Miami where she majored in international marketing and finance. She has been right alongside Johnson, supporting him through his multiple career transitions, from an aspiring professional football player, to star wrestler, and now actor/mogul. They share a daughter, Simone Alexandra Johnson. Dany asked for a divorce for no confirmed reason. Their divorce is considered to be one of the most amicable divorce settlements ever. They continue to be business partners.

Simone Alexandra Johnson: Simone is the oldest and only daughter of Dwayne and Dany. Simone has always been under the spotlight because of the popularity of her parents. She made headlines on her own for the first time when she was signed by ‘IMG Models,’ an international leader in talent acquisition and model management.

Lauren Hashian (partner 2007-present): Hashian and Dwayne have been dating since 2007. Lauren Hashian developed a passion for music and became a singer and songwriter. They share two daughters, Jasmine and Tiana Gia Johnson.

Jasmine Johnson: Jasmine is the oldest daughter of Dwayne and Lauren. She is 3 years old.

Tiana Gia Johnson: Tiana is the youngest daughter of Dwayne and Lauren. She is 1 year old.
