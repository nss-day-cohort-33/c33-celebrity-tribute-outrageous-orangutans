Dwayne Johnson

Career

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson had a promising football career that was cut short due to a number of injuries. It was not over for him, however. He joined the WWF in 1996, a year after graduating from the University of Miami. In a little over 10 years of professional wrestling, 'The Rock' was launched into Superstardom. Everybody knows his catchphrase, "Can you smell what the Roockk isss Cooookinnn?!"

The Rock did not fully settle with his mega famous wrestling career. In 1999, Dwayne began his acting debut. His acting career had a relatively slow start in small television roles. The rock kept at it, and within a couple of years he was starring in major Hollywood movies. Over the past 20 years, Dwayne Johnson has been in at least 44 movies! He was even the second highest paid actor in the world in 2018, following George Clooney.

Skills and Talents

  • Entertainment - 'The Rock' entertained thousands of people for over a decade as a wrestling showman
